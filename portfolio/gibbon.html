BIO-INSPIRED TRANSPORTER TOY

Fall 2012 | 5 Person Project

For this project, I worked in a team of five to build a bio-inspired toy for fourth graders. Through the course of the project, I went through the design process, starting from learning about the user group to iterating through mechanical prototypes. I learned a lot about project scheduling and working on a large team.
After interviewing our user group, fourth graders, we came up with a set of design goals for our project. Once we had the design goals, we began ideating and creating sketch models for our project. The image shows a sketch model for our gearing made out of LEGOs. Then we went through multiple iterations of laser cut delrin parts, until we had a working toy. My role on the team was specifically designing the leg subsystem, designing the monkey bar structure, and working on fabrication. While our project was ultimately unsuccessful, I learned a lot about the design process and working on teams on long term projects.
