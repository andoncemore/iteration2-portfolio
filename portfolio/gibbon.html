Design of a Bio Inspired Transporter Toy

Fall 2012 | 5 Person Project

Quick Read

For this project, I worked on a team of five to design and prototype a transporter toy based on an animal for fourth graders. Despite the fact that our final prototype was a failure, through the project process, I gained valuable experience using the design process, working through failure, and working on self-directed schedule.
