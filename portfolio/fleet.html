BRANDING A SHOE STARTUP

Spring 2013 | 4 Person Team

For this project, we designed and created a unpublished kickstarter for a modular shoe product. I specifically was the head of branding and graphic design for the project. I created posters, brochures, logos, and a landing page for our product.
This was our large size poster, which gives a general idea for what our product is and how it works. It is minimalistic and was used as an eye catching visual aid for a product booth. It uses the main product colors, and shows off the product.
The landing page was used to collect pre-orders for the project. It displays simple graphics to explain the project, a simple catch phrase, and a wide banner picture. It was again designed to be a simple and eye catching. It isn't content heavy, but gives basic details on the product.
The product brochure for this project has the most content for the project. The back gives details on how the actual product works, while the inside gives more specifics on the business side of the project. To see the full pdf version of the brochure, see the attached file.
