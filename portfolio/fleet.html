Creating and Developing a Shoe Startup

Spring 2013 | 4 Person Team

Quick Read

For this project, I worked in a four person team to create and develop a startup without actually creating a physical product for an Entrepreneurship class. The product that our startup, Fleet, made was a modular athletic shoe. We followed the lean startup methodology to make our initial concept of modular shoes into a more refined product. We then presented our final iteration and a simple prototype to various entreprenuers and investors.
